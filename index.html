About us

The Brooklyin Film festival is the biggest outdoor event in the city of New York USA, usually held annually at the beautiful Brooklyn Bridge Park and have people garther from over 20 cities in the United States to enjoy 4 days of amazing movies that appeals to audiences of all ages.

This year's event will be starting on the 5th of August and will end on the 8th of August, come with your family to get the best of experience, it's a free event, don't miss out this opportunity !!!

Organiser

Meet Jennifer Viala

Jennifer Viala is the founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. She is principally known as a big thing in the field of motion pictures. She recently won an award for the best cinematographer in New York, an award that puts her at a greater limelight than other sister-company owners. This festival is the biggest in New York and one of the most important in the world, an achievement that has been sustained for over 5 years now. She is the organizer of this year's Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival, she promises to make it a big one.
